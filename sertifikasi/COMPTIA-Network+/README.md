## Ringkasan Network+
Network+ memvalidasi keterampilan jaringan tingkat **entry‑to‑associate**.  
Direkomendasikan **CompTIA A+** dan **9–12 bulan** pengalaman jaringan.  
Ikhtisar & syarat: <https://www.comptia.org/en-us/certifications/network/>

---

## Format ujian & bahasa
Detail resmi: <https://www.comptia.org/en-us/certifications/network/>

- Seri ujian: **N10‑009 (V9)**.  
- Tanggal rilis: **20 Juni 2024**.  
- Jumlah soal: **maksimum 90** (MCQ + performance‑based).  
- Durasi: **90 menit**.  
- Skor lulus: **720** (skala **100–900**).  
- Bahasa: **English, German, Japanese, Portuguese, Spanish**.  
- Rekomendasi pengalaman: **A+** dan **9–12 bulan** peran jaringan.

## Apa yang dipelajari per domain
**1) Networking Concepts**  
OSI/TCP‑IP, perangkat jaringan, port & protokol, IPv4/IPv6, SDN/SD‑WAN.

**2) Network Implementation**  
Routing statis/dinamis, NAT/PAT, VLAN/STP, WLAN, instalasi fisik.

**3) Network Operations**  
Dokumentasi, konfigurasi, monitoring, DR (RPO/RTO), layanan jaringan.

**4) Network Security**  
Segmentasi, IAM/MFA, PKI, NAC, ancaman umum, hardening, compliance.

**5) Network Troubleshooting**  
Metodologi troubleshooting, isu kabel/performa, tools CLI, packet capture.

> Butir lengkap ada di **Exam Objectives**.  
> <https://comptiacdn.azureedge.net/webcontent/docs/default-source/exam-objectives/comptia-network-n10-009-exam-objectives-(4-0)-(1).pdf>

## Rencana belajar 6 minggu
**M1 — Konsep jaringan**  
Baca objectives end‑to‑end. Ringkas OSI, IP, port/protokol.

**M2 — Implementasi jaringan**  
Latih routing, VLAN, WLAN. Gambar topologi kecil.

**M3 — Operasi & layanan**  
Dokumentasi, backup config, monitoring, DR dasar.

**M4 — Keamanan jaringan**  
Segmentasi, NAC, PKI, hardening perangkat.

**M5 — Troubleshooting intensif**  
Latihan kasus, tcpdump/Wireshark, CLI tools.

**M6 — Review & simulasi**  
Dua simulasi 90 menit. Perbaiki gap terakhir.

## Setelah lulus: CEU & biaya CE
- **Kebutuhan renewal**: **30 CEUs** tiap **3 tahun** (Network+ V9).  
  <https://www.comptia.org/en-us/resources/ce/learn/earn-continuing-education-units-ceus/>  
  <https://www.comptia.org/en-us/resources/ce/renew-options/renewing-network-single/>
- **Biaya CE**: total **US$150/3 tahun** untuk Network+.  
  Dapat dibayar kapan saja dalam siklus.  
  <https://help.comptia.org/hc/en-us/articles/14382051258004-What-Are-the-Fees-to-Renew-My-Certification>
- **Opsi cepat**: **CertMaster CE** untuk Network+.  
  Syarat & pembelian:  
  <https://help.comptia.org/hc/en-us/articles/14378942258964-What-Are-the-Eligibility-Requirements-for-CertMaster-CE>  
  <https://help.comptia.org/hc/en-us/articles/14380197497108-How-to-Purchase-CertMaster-CE-for-Certification-Renewal>
- **Renew by exam**: ambil versi ujian terbaru; **CE fee di‑waive**.  
  <https://help.comptia.org/hc/en-us/articles/13923673767444-What-Are-the-Single-Activity-Renewal-Options>
- **Cascading renewal**: sertifikasi lebih tinggi dapat memperbarui yang lebih rendah.  
  <https://www.comptia.org/en-us/resources/ce/choose/renew-with-a-single-activity/earn-a-higher-level-comptia-certification/>

## Latihan cepat
- Buat lab dua subnet, satu VLAN trunk.  
- Uji OSPF single‑area dengan dua router virtual.  
- Atur DHCP + DNS lokal, dokumentasikan skema.  
- Tangkap handshake TCP dan analisis flags.  
- Simulasikan isu MTU dan perbaiki fragmentasi.  
- Terapkan ACL sederhana pada edge.
