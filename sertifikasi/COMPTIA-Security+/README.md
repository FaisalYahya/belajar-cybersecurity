## Ringkasan Security+
Security+ memvalidasi keterampilan inti keamanan siber tingkat **entry‑level**.  
Direkomendasikan memiliki **Network+** dan pengalaman **2 tahun** admin IT/security.  
Ikhtisar: <https://www.comptia.org/en-us/certifications/security/>

---

## Format ujian & bahasa
Rujukan resmi “Exam details”: <https://www.comptia.org/en-us/certifications/security/>

- Kode: **SY0‑701** (V7).  
- Jumlah soal: **maksimum 90** (MCQ + performance‑based).  
- Durasi: **90 menit**.  
- Skor lulus: **750** (skala **100–900**).  
- Bahasa: **English, Japanese, Portuguese, Spanish, Thai**.  
- Penyelenggara: **Pearson VUE** (test center **atau** online proctoring).

## Apa yang dipelajari per domain
**1) General Security Concepts**  
CIA/AAA, kontrol keamanan, change management, dasar kriptografi.

**2) Threats, Vulnerabilities, and Mitigations**  
Aktor ancaman, vektor serangan, kerentanan, teknik mitigasi.

**3) Security Architecture**  
Model arsitektur, infrastruktur enterprise, proteksi data, *resilience*.

**4) Security Operations**  
Baseline & hardening, wireless/app security, vuln management, monitoring, IAM, otomasi, IR & forensik.

**5) Security Program Management and Oversight**  
Governance, risk, third‑party risk, compliance, audit/assessment, awareness.

> Detail butir lengkap ada di **Exam Objectives (PDF)**.  
> <https://assets.ctfassets.net/82ripq7fjls2/6TYWUym0Nudqa8nGEnegjG/0f9b974d3b1837fe85ab8e6553f4d623/CompTIA-Security-Plus-SY0-701-Exam-Objectives.pdf>

## Rencana belajar 6 minggu
**M1 — Konsep & Risiko**  
Baca objectives end‑to‑end. Ringkas CIA/AAA, kontrol, kripto dasar.

**M2 — Ancaman & Mitigasi**  
Peta aktor & vektor. Latih analisis skenario dan prioritas kontrol.

**M3 — Arsitektur**  
Segmentasi, zero‑trust, proteksi data, *HA/DR*. Buat diagram arsitektur.

**M4 — Operasi Keamanan (1)**  
Baseline, hardening, wireless/app security. Praktik vuln scanning.

**M5 — Operasi Keamanan (2)**  
Monitoring, log, IAM, IR, *forensics* dasar. Simulasi insiden mini.

**M6 — Program & Review**  
Governance, risk, compliance. Dua simulasi 90 menit. *Gap‑fix*.

## Latihan cepat
- Ringkas tiap domain dalam satu halaman.  
- Buat 20 soal latihan per domain.  
- Simulasikan **90 menit** tanpa jeda.  
- Review jawaban salah dan rujuk objectives.  
- Tulis *cheat sheet* port, protokol, & kontrol utama.
