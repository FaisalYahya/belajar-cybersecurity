## Tujuan modul

* Menemukan opsi **gratis** yang kredibel.
* Menentukan urutan belajar efektif.
* Menyiapkan portofolio dan verifikasi.

---

## Cakupan & definisi

* **Certification**: ujian proktor, kredensial industri.
* **Certificate of Completion**: bukti selesai kursus.
* **Digital badge**: kredensial terverifikasi online.

---

## Entry‑level global gratis

**(ISC)² Certified in Cybersecurity (CC)**

* Pelatihan + **voucher ujian gratis** via One Million CC.
* Setelah lulus, AMF **US$50** per tahun.
* Daftar: [ISC2 One Million CC](https://www.isc2.org/landing/1mcc)

## Program vendor gratis

**Fortinet — NSE 1–2 (Network Security Fundamentals)**

* Kursus self‑paced gratis + sertifikat.
* Info: [Fortinet NSE Training](https://www.fortinet.com/nse-training)
* Pengumuman: [Fortinet Free Training Blog](https://www.fortinet.com/blog/business-and-technology/fortinet-offers-free-cybersecurity-training-courses)

**Cisco — Networking Academy & Ethical Hacking**

* Kursus gratis: Intro to Cybersecurity, Essentials, dst.
* Program offensive gratis: **Cisco Certificate in Ethical Hacking**.
* Tautan: [Cisco Networking Academy](https://www.netacad.com/) · [Cisco Ethical Hacking Certificate](https://www.cisco.com/site/us/en/learn/training-certifications/certifications/ethical-hacker/index.html) · [Blog Resmi](https://blogs.cisco.com/learning/hack-your-way-to-a-new-career-in-cybersecurity)

**Security Blue Team — BTJA**

* Selesaikan 6 kursus gratis → **BTJA certificate**.
* Panduan: [Cara Mendapat BTJA](https://support.securityblue.team/hc/en-gb/articles/16796664331164-How-to-Obtain-the-BTJA-Certificate)
* Sertifikat per kursus: [Free Course Certificates](https://support.securityblue.team/hc/en-gb/articles/11308786817436-Certificate-of-Completion-Free-courses)

**Palo Alto Networks — Fundamental tracks**

* Kursus fundamental gratis di Beacon.
* Lihat: [Free Cybersecurity Education Courses](https://www.paloaltonetworks.com/cyberpedia/free-cybersecurity-education-courses)

**Splunk — Academic Alliance**

* Pelatihan gratis untuk akademik; kadang ada voucher.
* Lihat: [Splunk Academic Alliance](https://www.splunk.com/en_us/resources/splunk-academic-alliance.html)

**IBM — SkillsBuild**

* **Cybersecurity Fundamentals** + digital badge gratis.
* Lihat: [IBM SkillsBuild Cybersecurity](https://skillsbuild.org/students/course-catalog/cybersecurity)

**EC‑Council — CodeRed “Essentials”**

* NDE / EHE / DFE gratis + sertifikat.
* Lihat: [EC‑Council Free Courses for Beginners](https://www.eccouncil.org/cybersecurity-exchange/cyber-novice/free-cybersecurity-courses-beginners/)

**freeCodeCamp — Information Security**

* Kurikulum web security berbasis proyek.
* Sertifikat gratis: [freeCodeCamp Information Security](https://www.freecodecamp.org/learn/information-security)

## Platform hands‑on dan badge

**TryHackMe**

* Banyak learning path gratis + **Certificate of Completion**.
* Info: [Certificate vs Certification](https://help.tryhackme.com/en/articles/10625009-certificate-of-completion-vs-professional-certification)

**Hack The Box (HTB)**

* Lab eksploitasi dan CTF; modul dasar gratis di HTB Academy.
* Lihat: [Hack The Box](https://www.hackthebox.com/) · [HTB Academy](https://academy.hackthebox.com/)

## Program Indonesia

**Kominfo — Digital Talent Scholarship (DTS)**

* Akademi gratis; peluang **sertifikasi BNSP** di jalur tertentu.
* Resmi: [Digital Talent Scholarship](https://digitalent.kominfo.go.id/)

**BSSN — Akademi & event komunitas**

* Workshop/webinar gratis dengan sertifikat partisipasi.
* Info: [BSSN](https://bssn.go.id/)

## Rencana 30 hari

**Minggu 1**

* Daftar **One Million CC**.
* Selesaikan **Fortinet NSE 1–2**.

**Minggu 2**

* Tuntaskan **Cisco Intro to Cybersecurity**.
* Mulai **TryHackMe Pre‑Security**.

**Minggu 3**

* Selesaikan 2 kursus **SBT eLearning**.
* Lanjut **TryHackMe Complete Beginner**.

**Minggu 4**

* Pilih **IBM SkillsBuild** atau **CodeRed Essentials**.
* Klaim **BTJA** bila syarat terpenuhi.

---

## Klaim & verifikasi sertifikat

* Simpan **ID verifikasi** dan URL resmi.
* Pasang badge di **Credly** atau LinkedIn.
* Jelaskan level: certificate vs certification.
* Sertakan **tahun** dan **tautan verifikasi**.

---

## Latihan cepat

* Klaim voucher **ISC2 CC** dan buat jadwal.
* Lulus **NSE 1–2** akhir pekan ini.
* Tuntaskan satu path **TryHackMe**.
* Selesaikan satu **SBT course** per hari.
* Unggah semua badge ke **LinkedIn**.

---

## Checklist ringkas

* [ ] Daftar **One Million CC**.
* [ ] Lulus **NSE 1–2** dan unduh sertifikat.
* [ ] Selesaikan kursus **Cisco/IBM** gratis.
* [ ] Tuntaskan 6 kursus **SBT** → **BTJA**.
* [ ] Lengkapi portofolio dengan link verifikasi.
