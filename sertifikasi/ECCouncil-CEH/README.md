## Ringkasan CEH

  <https://www.eccouncil.org/train-certify/certified-ethical-hacker-ceh/>

## Format ujian & jalur kredensial

**CEH MCQ (Knowledge Exam)**  
- **4 jam**, **125 soal** pilihan ganda.  
- Kode ujian: **312‑50 (ECC/VUE)**.  
- Passing score **variabel 60%–85%** per form.  
- Rujukan resmi:  
  <https://iclass.eccouncil.org/our-courses/certified-ethical-hacker-ceh/>  
  <https://www.eccouncil.org/certified-ethical-hacker-online-training/>

**CEH Practical (Hands‑on)**  
- **6 jam**, **20 tantangan** di **cyber range**.  
- Validasi keterampilan nyata, bukan simulasi.  
- Rujukan:  
  <https://www.eccouncil.org/train-certify/certified-ethical-hacker-ceh-practical/>  
  <https://cert.eccouncil.org/certified-ethical-hacker-practical.html>

**CEH Master**  
- Dicapai bila lulus **CEH MCQ** **dan** **CEH Practical**.  
- Rujukan: <https://www.eccouncil.org/train-certify/ceh-master/>

## Topik & capaian belajar
- **Fase ethical hacking**: footprinting, scanning, gaining access, maintaining access, covering tracks.  
- **Teknik & domain**: rekayasa sosial, sniffing, DoS, web app, SQLi, Wi‑Fi, mobile, IoT/OT, cloud.  
- **Operasional**: enumerasi, privilege escalation, pivoting, exfiltration, anti‑forensics.  
- **Komponen AI di CEH v13**: automasi analisis, deteksi berbasis AI, **hacking AI systems**.  
  <https://www.eccouncil.org/train-certify/certified-ethical-hacker-ceh/>

## Rencana belajar 6 minggu

**M1 — Dasar & metodologi**  
- Baca halaman CEH resmi dan FAQ.  
- Ringkas lima fase serangan.  

**M2 — Rekon & scanning**  
- Latih OSINT, DNS, port scanning, layanan.  
- Catat teknik fingerprinting OS.  

**M3 — Eksploitasi host & jaringan**  
- Latih gaining/maintaining access di lab.  
- Praktik privilege escalation.  

**M4 — Web & cloud**  
- Uji OWASP Top 10 secara aman.  
- Coba serangan dasar pada layanan cloud lab.  

**M5 — Forensik ringan & anti‑forensics**  
- Logging, jejak artefak, dan *covering tracks*.  
- Kerjakan **challenge** bergaya Practical.  

**M6 — Review & simulasi**  
- Simulasi **4 jam/125 soal**.  
- Uji **20 tantangan** bergaya Practical.

> [!WARNING]
> Uji hanya pada lab pribadi atau sistem berizin.  
> Patuhi hukum dan kebijakan setempat.

## Pendaftaran, eligibility, dan penjadwalan
- **Eligibility tanpa training**: butuh **≥2 tahun** pengalaman InfoSec **atau** ikuti training resmi.  
  Aplikasi tanpa training **US$100**.  
  <https://cert.eccouncil.org/application-process-eligibility.html>  
- **Voucher CEH**: ECC Exam **US$950**, Pearson VUE **US$1199** (untuk kandidat training resmi).  
  <https://cert.eccouncil.org/application-process-eligibility.html>  
- **Penjadwalan**:  
  ECC Exam Center / RPS: <https://www.eccexam.com>  
  Pearson VUE: <https://www.pearsonvue.com/us/en/eccouncil.html>  
  Info test center: <https://cert.eccouncil.org/test-centers.html>

## Latihan cepat
- Bangun lab isolasi dengan 2 VM target.  
- Lakukan OSINT domain fiktif dan dokumentasi.  
- Uji serangan web aman di DVWA/Juice Shop.  
- Tangkap trafik dan analisis *pcap* sederhana.  
- Selesaikan 5 CTF singkat bergaya CEH.  
- Tulis *report* satu halaman tiap latihan.
