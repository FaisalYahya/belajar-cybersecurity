# 1. Kenapa Compliance / Audit / Risk / Legal Cocok Masuk Cybersecurity?

Karena latar belakang ini sangat beririsan dengan area security strategis:

- Memahami regulasi, kebijakan, dan standar ‚Üí sangat penting dalam cyber governance
- Terbiasa membuat policy & SOP ‚Üí diperlukan untuk security governance
- Paham audit, kontrol, risk assessment ‚Üí inti dari cyber risk management
- Familiar dengan kerangka kepatuhan ‚Üí ISO 27001, SOC 2, GDPR, PCI-DSS
- Mampu dokumentasi formal & komunikasi ‚Üí sangat dibutuhkan oleh GRC

‚ö° **Fun Fact:**  
Banyak GRC lead, CISO, cyber program manager berasal dari **audit atau compliance**, bukan teknis.

# 3. Roadmap Skill Development

## Step 1 ‚Äî Cybersecurity Foundations (Non-Technical Friendly)
Pelajari konsep dasar:

- CIA Triad
- Zero Trust Principles
- Defense-in-depth
- Common cyber threats
- Incident lifecycle

**Resources:**
- Google Cybersecurity Certificate: https://www.coursera.org/professional-certificates/google-cybersecurity
- SANS Glossary: https://www.sans.org/security-resources/glossary/

---

## Step 2 ‚Äî Governance & Framework Mastery
Fokus pada:

- **ISO 27001** (most important)
- **NIST CSF**
- **SOC 2**
- **COBIT**
- **PCI-DSS** (jika fintech)

Pahami:

- Security controls
- Control objectives
- Gap assessment
- Auditing methodology

**Resources:**
- ISO 27001 Overview ‚Äî https://www.iso.org/isoiec-27001-information-security.html  
- NIST Cybersecurity Framework ‚Äî https://www.nist.gov/cyberframework  

---

## Step 3 ‚Äî Risk Management
Belajar:

- Risk assessment methodology (ISO 27005 / NIST 800-30)
- Likelihood vs impact scoring
- KRIs, KPIs, control effectiveness
- Risk register management

Tools:

- Excel risk register templates
- GRC tools (OneTrust, Archer, Jira-based GRC)

---

## Step 4 ‚Äî Cyber Compliance Operations
Pelajari:

- SOC 2 Trust Principles
- GDPR/PDPA compliance
- Vendor risk questionnaires
- Data classification
- DPIA / PIA (Privacy Impact Assessments)
- Regulatory mapping (OJK, HIPAA, PCI, dll.)

---

## Step 5 ‚Äî Policy, Standards & Procedure Writing
Wajib untuk GRC roles:

- Information security policy
- Access control policy
- Data retention policy
- Incident response plan
- Business continuity policy
- Vendor management policy

---

## Step 6 ‚Äî Exposure to Technical Domains (Opsional)
Walau tidak wajib, pemahaman berikut membantu:

- What is SIEM
- Basic network/endpoint concepts
- Types of security logs
- Vulnerability scanning concepts

**Resources:**
- MITRE ATT&CK: https://attack.mitre.org
